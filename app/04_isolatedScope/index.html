Scope vs Isolated scope

  • AngularJS has a service called $rootScope.
  • The $rootScope is used as the compilation context by the $compile service in the angular.bootstrap function.
  • The $rootScope is a tree that can grow with its $new method.
  • The $new method returns a child scope or a child isolated scope according its arguments.
  • Batarang helps you to check the $scope when needed.

Child Scope

  • When the scope does not know the property, it asks to its parent to give it.
  • When the scope set a property, it will not ask again to its parent.

Isolated Scope

  • When the scope does not know the property, it DOES NOT ask to its parent to give it.
